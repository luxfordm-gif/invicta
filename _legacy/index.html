Private Water Supplies

Independent water,
designed around your source.

A private water supply gives your property genuine independence. Reliable, high-quality water from your own source — free of rising utility charges, unaffected by mains failures, and treated to a standard mains water rarely matches.

The catch: every source is different, and doing it properly is not a job for generalists. We only work on bespoke systems, designed around your specific source water, installed to run quietly for decades, and personally maintained for as long as you need us.

Frequently asked.

The questions we hear most often from clients thinking about a private supply. If yours isn't answered here, get in touch — we'll answer plainly.

Do I need permission to install a borehole?

In most cases, no formal planning permission is required, though water abstraction over 20 cubic metres per day requires a licence from the Environment Agency. We handle the paperwork and liaison as part of any install where it's needed.

How long does a borehole and treatment system last?

A properly designed and installed system will run for 25–40 years with routine maintenance. Filtration media and UV lamps are consumables replaced on a service schedule. The borehole itself, the pump housing, and the treatment vessels are all long-life.

How often does the water need testing?

Annually as standard, though we recommend more frequent testing if your source water is known to be variable. All testing goes through a UKAS-accredited laboratory.

What happens if something goes wrong outside working hours?

Existing service clients have direct access to our engineers on a 24-hour emergency line. In most cases we can get to site within four hours of the call.

Can you take over servicing a system installed by someone else?

Yes — provided the system is safe to work on and we can access the plant. We start with a full inspection and water analysis, then propose any remedial work needed before taking on the ongoing service contract.

What does a typical install cost?

Every install is bespoke, so there is no typical figure. Costs depend on source depth, water chemistry, storage requirements and property demand. We provide a fixed-price quote after a site survey.
